A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India, serving from 2002 to 2007.
A career scientist turned politician, Kalam was educated at the Madras Institute of
Technology and later at the Indian Institute of Technology.
He was a key scientist in the development of India's first indigenous satellite launch vehicle,
the SLV-III,and the first indigenous ballistic missile, the Prithvi.
